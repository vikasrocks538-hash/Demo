BDS MDS – Prosthodontics BPTH MPT – Orthopaedics PGDCC

Dr. Abdc is a highly qualified clinician holding dual specialisations in Prosthodontics (Dental) and Orthopaedic Physiotherapy. With more than 10 years of clinical experience, the doctor has helped thousands of patients regain their health, confidence, and quality of life.

Known for combining the latest clinical evidence with a compassionate, patient-first approach, Dr. Abdc ensures that every individual who walks through the door receives personalised attention and the highest standard of care.

The clinic is equipped with modern diagnostic tools and treatment facilities to address a wide spectrum of dental and musculoskeletal concerns — all under one roof.
